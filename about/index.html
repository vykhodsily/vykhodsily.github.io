About Vykhod Sily

«Выход Силы» or «Vykhod Sily» (or loosely «the way the Force goes through» in English) is:

  • A podcast launched by Rustee (Special Request Crew) back in 2013 to push 85/170 BPM things. Aired/uploaded every Thursday on radio.dropthebass.ru 8 pm (GMT+3).
  • A worldwide community of the listeners, producers, well known dj’s & bedroom selectors teamed up under the Vykhod Sily banner on the internet platforms and social media.
  • A digital label launched in 2017 focused on dark and deep 85/170 BPM electronica. Supported through the crowd-sourcing campaign: patreon.com/vykhodsily_podcast.
