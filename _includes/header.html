Podcast “Vykhod Sily”

                       

“Vykhod Sily” (or something like “Force Output” in English) - tribal, ethno, dubby deep halftempo sounds of today electronic music within the 85/170 BPM range represented by Rustee (Special Request Crew). An onward intensive movement into the depths of massive and heavy halfstep sound which normally takes one hour every week at djstation.ru and Mixlr on Thursdays (22:00 UTC +5).
